Solute transport
================

Solute transport is implemented with StorAge selection (SAS) functions:

.. math::
  C_Q=\int_{T=0}^{\infty} C_S(T,t) \cdot \alpha_p \cdot \overleftarrow{p}_{Q}(T,t) dT
